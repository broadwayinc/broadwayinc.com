Article is a text content

The HTML <article> element represents a content block that is part of a document or site.

You may use various styled text tags to represent the content.

When vertically centering things...

You may use flexbox or tables to center things vertically.

Don't forget to calculate the height of the header.

Also, consider NOT vertically centering things on page

1. It may not look good on mobile devices,
2. Actually, it may look/read better to use some margin on top.
